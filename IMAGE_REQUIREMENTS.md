Use this as the media checklist for a professional launch.

- `public/assets/brand/logo-light.svg` (for dark backgrounds)
- `public/assets/brand/logo-dark.svg` (for light backgrounds)
- `public/assets/brand/favicon-512.png`
- `public/assets/brand/favicon-192.png`

## 2) Hero Section

## 6) Optional Gallery (recommended)

8 to 12 real lab photos:

- Field/lab setup
- Wearables/sensors in use
- Team collaboration
- Conference/poster presentations
- Classroom/training scenes

Suggested format: `public/assets/gallery/<slug>-2000x1333.jpg`

## Quality standards

- Prefer real lab/team photos over generic stock images.
- Keep color tone consistent across all photos.
- Export JPG/WebP for photos, SVG for logos/icons.
- Target size:
  - Hero: 300 KB to 700 KB
  - Cards/team images: 120 KB to 300 KB
- Always include descriptive alt text in HTML for accessibility.
